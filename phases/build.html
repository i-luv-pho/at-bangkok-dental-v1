Overview

The Build Phase

Building is where ideas become reality. After validating that a real problem exists and customers will pay to solve it, teams move into execution mode. This is where the rubber meets the road.

Students don't just build products—they build companies. This includes formal business registration, setting up accounting systems, establishing operations processes, and all the unglamorous work that separates side projects from real businesses.

Core Principle

Ship something imperfect today rather than something perfect never. Real learning comes from real customers using real products.

Teams receive seed funding of 50,000 THB to launch their ventures. This is real capital with real expectations. The stakes create accountability and force difficult prioritization decisions that define entrepreneurship.

Activities

What You'll Do

01

MVP Development

Build the minimum viable product—the smallest version that delivers value. Learn to scope ruthlessly and ship quickly. Perfect is the enemy of shipped.

02

Company Registration

Register your business as a legal entity. Understand corporate structure, equity distribution, and the legal foundations of a real company.

03

Operations Setup

Establish the systems needed to run a business: accounting, project management, customer support, and internal communication processes.

04

Resource Management

Manage your seed funding effectively. Make difficult tradeoff decisions about where to invest limited resources for maximum impact.
