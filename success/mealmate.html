TED Fund Ideation Award

Team MealMate, second-year DDI students from MSME ABAC, won 100,000 baht from the TED Fund Ideation for their innovative supplement drink designed to boost appetite in the elderly.

Using natural herbs, MealMate enhances taste perception, aids swallowing, and improves digestion - addressing a critical health challenge facing Thailand's aging population. The solution combines traditional knowledge of Thai herbs with modern food technology.
