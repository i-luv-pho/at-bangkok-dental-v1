iReadCustomer is transforming how businesses understand their customers. Using Agentic AI technology, the platform turns the "voice of the customer" into deep, actionable insights faster than traditional research methods while eliminating heavy manual work.

Starting as a DDI Sandbox project, Team Starters identified a critical pain point in market research: the time and effort required to analyze customer feedback. Their AI-driven solution processes customer data at scale, delivering insights that previously took weeks in just hours.
