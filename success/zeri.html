Team Zeri emerged from DDI's first cohort with a mission to revolutionize hormone health through natural solutions. Founded by DDI students, Zeri Well Plus Co., Ltd. develops hormone-balancing supplements derived from natural Thai herbs, addressing a growing market need with innovative, science-backed products.

Their journey from student startup to funded company demonstrates the power of DDI's entrepreneurship-focused curriculum and industry mentorship program.
