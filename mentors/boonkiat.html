About Saha Group

Saha Group

Thailand's largest consumer products conglomerate, comprising approximately 300 companies.

Saha Group manufactures and distributes over 30,000 products across categories including detergents, toothpaste, cosmetics, instant noodles, beverages, apparel, and textiles. The group operates 80 joint ventures with Japanese partners including Lion, Wacoal, Kewpie, Gunze, Otsuka, Lawson, and S.T. Corporation.

Listed subsidiaries under the group generated 102.6 billion baht ($3.3 billion USD) in sales in 2020. The primary holding company, Saha Pathana Inter-Holding (SPI), is publicly listed on the Stock Exchange of Thailand. The group also operates four industrial parks in Thailand.

Dr. Boonkiat Chokwattana is part of the Chokwatana family, who are major shareholders of Saha Group.
