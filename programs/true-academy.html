The Program

Learn by Doing at True

4-5

Months Internship

True Academy is DDI's flagship industry partnership program with True Corporation, Thailand's leading telecommunications and digital services company. This isn't a typical internship—it's a comprehensive professional development program where students take on real responsibilities and contribute to actual business operations.

Students work alongside True's professionals on real projects, gaining hands-on experience in digital innovation, technology, and business operations while earning academic credits.
