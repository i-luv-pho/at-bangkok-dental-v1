QUALIFICATIONS

Thai Students

  • High school graduates (Mathayom 6)
  • Students awaiting graduation are eligible
  • Any equivalent certificate approved by Ministry of Education

International Qualifications

  • IGCSE/GCSE/GCE "O" Level: Min 5 subjects with "C" grade + GCE "A" or "AS" Level: Min 3 subjects with "C" grade
  • GED: Min 145 per subject, total min 580
  • IB Diploma: Min 5 subjects with Grade 4
  • Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF) certified
  • Other Ministry of Education approved certificates
