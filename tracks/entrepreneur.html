Overview

Build Your Business

The Entrepreneur Track provides a structured pathway for students whose Sandbox ventures show strong potential. Continue developing your business with dedicated faculty mentorship and a customized academic curriculum designed to support your specific business goals.

This pathway allows you to maintain academic progress while focusing the majority of your efforts on growing your startup into a sustainable business.
